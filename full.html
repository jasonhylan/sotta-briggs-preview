Practice Areas

We focus our practice on the areas of law that matter most to individuals and families in Southwest Missouri.

Criminal Defense

Aggressive defense for misdemeanor and felony charges, DWI/DUI, drug offenses, assault, theft, and traffic violations. We protect your rights at every stage — from arraignment through trial.

Family Law

Compassionate guidance through divorce, child custody, child support, modifications, adoptions, and paternity matters. We help families navigate difficult transitions with clarity and care.

Estate Planning

Wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and probate administration tailored to protect your family's future. We make the process straightforward and ensure your wishes are carried out.

Our Firm

A Tradition of Trusted Counsel

The Law Office of Sotta & Briggs has been a cornerstone of legal practice in Jasper County for more than 30 years. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves skilled, accessible representation, our firm has helped thousands of clients navigate the complexities of the legal system.

Attorney Bob Briggs brings decades of courtroom experience and a deep familiarity with the local judicial landscape. From the Jasper County Courthouse to the Missouri Court of Appeals, we have the knowledge and relationships to advocate effectively on your behalf.

Whether you're facing criminal charges, going through a divorce, or planning your estate, we provide the personalized attention and strategic counsel your case demands.
